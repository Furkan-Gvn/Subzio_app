Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Subzio ("we", "our", or "the App") is a subscription management application developed by Furkan. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Subscription details you enter (name, cost, billing cycle, category)
  • Notification preferences
  • App settings and language preferences

All subscription data is stored locally on your device only using on-device storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal subscription data on any external server.

2. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob:

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App.
  • AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications to remind you about upcoming subscription payments. These notifications are generated and managed entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

4. Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's own security measures. We recommend using device-level security features such as screen locks and encryption.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual subscriptions within the App
  • Clearing the App's data from your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
